= Project Vision
:description: Mission, target audience, scope, success criteria, identity, and analysis methodology.

== Mission

Make French legislation accessible and understandable to the general public by transforming dense legal texts into clear, visual summaries published on Instagram.

== Problem

French citizens face a dual gap:

. *Awareness:* most citizens never learn that new laws have been passed.
. *Comprehension:* even when they do encounter legislation, the legal language is impenetrable.

Lexigram bridges both gaps by bringing digestible summaries to where people already spend time.

== Target Audience

The general French public -- everyday citizens who want to understand new laws affecting their lives.

== Channel

Instagram is the deliberate and sole distribution channel -- not a stepping stone to multi-platform.

== Scope

The vision encompasses all impactful legislative texts: laws, decrees, ordinances.
The current implementation starts with laws (`LOI`) only -- phased approach.

== Success Criteria

[%header,cols="1,2"]
|===
|Criterion |Definition

|*Coverage*
|Every new legislative text gets summarized and published -- no gaps.

|*Comprehension quality*
|Summaries are accurate, accessible, and genuinely help people understand the law.
|===

Engagement metrics (likes, followers) are secondary.
The mission is coverage and quality, not virality.

== Identity

Lexigram is a *civic utility with an educational tone* -- a neutral, informative public service that empowers citizens.
Not opinionated, not sensationalist.

== Analysis Methodology

=== Citizen-First Framing

The analysis leads with "how does this affect you" for citizen-impacting laws.
For institutional laws (treaties, parliamentary procedures, government reorganization), the framing adapts to informational -- no forced citizen-impact angle.

The agent adapts its lens based on the nature of the law.

=== Delta-Aware Analysis

For *amending laws* (laws that modify existing legislation), the analysis highlights what changed versus the previous version.
The pipeline uses `TexteLoda.format_modifications_report()` from pylegifrance as input to identify modifications, creations, and abrogations.

For *standalone new laws*, the analysis focuses on impact explanation without a delta.

=== Merged Format

A single analysis weaves key legal facts (title, date, context) with the impact explanation.
Not a separate legal breakdown followed by a citizen summary -- one integrated narrative.

=== No Advice

Lexigram informs and explains.
It never recommends action or tells people what to do.

=== Evolution Path

Future enhancement: use `TexteLoda.get_modified_articles()` combined with `Code.fetch_article().at(date)` to fetch actual before/after article text for deep article-level comparison.
