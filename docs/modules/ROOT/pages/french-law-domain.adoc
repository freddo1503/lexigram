= French Law Domain
:description: French legislative process, JORF, LOI, LEGITEXT identifiers, legal terms glossary.

Domain knowledge for understanding how French legislation works and how Lexigram interacts with it.

== How French Laws Are Published

. *Parliament votes* a law (Assemblee nationale + Senat)
. The *President signs* the law (promulgation)
. The law is *published in the JORF* (Journal Officiel de la Republique Francaise)
. *Legifrance* indexes the law and makes it searchable via API

Lexigram processes laws at step 4 -- after they appear in the Legifrance database.

== Legifrance Identifiers

[%header,cols="1,2,2"]
|===
|ID Type |Format |Example

|LEGITEXT |Unique text identifier |`LEGITEXT000051827204`
|JORF |Journal Officiel reference |Used in source URLs
|Lexigram textId |Composite key |`LEGITEXT000051827204_02-07-2025` (ID + publication date)
|===

The composite `textId` format enables date-based sorting of laws in DynamoDB.
The LEGITEXT portion is extracted for Legifrance API calls.

== Law Structure

A French law (`LOI`) typically contains:

* *Title:* Official name (e.g., "Loi n 2025-XXX du ...")
* *Signatories:* President, Prime Minister, relevant ministers
* *Articles:* Individual provisions, numbered sequentially
* *Publication date:* Date of publication in the JORF

== Types of Legal Texts

[%header,cols="1,1,2"]
|===
|French |English |Description

|LOI |Law |Voted by Parliament. *This is what Lexigram processes* (`natures=["LOI"]`).
|Decret |Decree |Executive order by the government. Not processed.
|Ordonnance |Ordinance |Government measure with force of law. Not processed.
|Arrete |Ministerial order |Decision by a minister. Not processed.
|===

== Glossary

[%header,cols="1,1,2"]
|===
|Term |Translation |Context in Lexigram

|Assemblee nationale |National Assembly |Lower house of French Parliament
|Senat |Senate |Upper house of French Parliament
|Promulgation |Promulgation |Presidential act of signing a law into effect
|JORF |Official Journal |Where laws are officially published
|Legifrance |Legifrance |Government website and API for legal information
|Signataires |Signatories |Officials who signed the law
|Article |Article |Individual clause/section within a law
|LODA |Laws, Ordinances, Decrees, Orders |The fund (collection) in Legifrance that Lexigram queries
|===
